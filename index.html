About Me

I graduated from MIT in 2024 as a double major S.B. in Mathematics (course 18) and Computer Science, Economics, and Data Science (course 6-14) with a M.Eng. in Computer Science, Economics, and Data Science. I then was a Part III student, receiving a MASt in Pure Mathematics from the University of Cambridge, at Trinity College, graduating with Distinction in 2025. I will begin my PhD at Stanford in the Department of Mathematics in September 2025.

My research interests include probability, combinatorics, and discrete geometry.
